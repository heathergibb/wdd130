My Family

Curtis and I were married in the Carston, Alberta Temple on May 21, 1998, and since then have added four wonderful children to our family and are looking forward to future additions as they grow, get married and start having their own families.

Curtis

Curtis is a supportive husband and father who loves Lego, movies, Star Wars (the originals), and enjoys camping and hiking in the mountains. He recently started remote work as an Electrical Designer for Wood PLC. Before that, he worked as a Technical Resources Technologist for ATCO Electric.

Talia

Talia is a delightful and friendly gal who enjoys hanging out with friends while attending the University of Lethbridge. She is in her 4th year of a 5-year combined B.A./B.Ed. Degree. She plans to work as an elementary school teacher. Like her dad, she loves Lego and movies, mostly Marvel movies. Like her mom, she appreciates good music and nature. One of her happiest places is the mountains.

Alex

Alex is an easy-going and funny guy who brings light and humor to our family through gentle teasing and positive energy. He is currently serving a mission in South Carolina and plans to get a Computer Science degree following his mission. He loves playing computer games and spending time with his friends.

Maisie

Maisie is a quiet girl with a strong spirit. She is an example of quiet determination and strength, who like her sister, wants to be an elementary school teacher. She loves children, especially babies and has a calm, nurturing personality that puts them at ease around her. She enjoys writing in various journals and notebooks and watching YouTube.

Eli

Eli is an energic and adventurous young man who loves the outdoors and animals, especially dogs. He enjoyed trying some new things this year including; canoeing, fishing, hunting, tubing, dirt-biking and eating wild game. He also enjoys nature and camping in the mountains.
